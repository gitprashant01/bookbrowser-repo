📘 About Book Browser

Discover | Save | Empower.

Book Browser is a modern web application built for readers who crave simplicity, speed, and smart discovery. Whether you're exploring timeless classics or trending titles, our platform helps you search, save, and export your favorite books with ease.

🌐 What We Do
  • 🔍 Smart Search: Find books by title, author, or keyword instantly
  • ⭐ Favorites: Save and manage your personal reading list
  • 📤 Export to CSV: Download your collection for sharing or archiving
  • 🛒 Affiliate Links: Discover books and buy them directly via Amazon
 💡 Why Book Browser?

We believe reading should be effortless and enjoyable. Book Browser is designed with a user-first mindset— fast, responsive, and built with clean UI/UX principles. Whether you're a student, educator, or lifelong learner, our app helps you stay organized and inspired.

🛠️ Built with Passion

Book Browser is developed using Angular, Material UI, and free book APIs. It’s a project born from curiosity, refined through iteration, and driven by a commitment to empower users through technology.
